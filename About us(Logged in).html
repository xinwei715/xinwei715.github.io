Northern Fame Bookstore

Northern Fame Bookstore (NFB) is a Malaysia local retail and distributor bookstore that selling all kinds of books to customers which is located at Bukit Jalil, Malaysia. In the NFB, they allow customers to walk-in to their store and buy or borrow books. NFB also accepts to receive email orders from customer and deliver the books to the customers’ places as their one of the service.


Address:12-1, First Floor, Block 5, Jalil Link, Jalan Jalil Jaya 6, Bukit Jalil,Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Operation time: 10 A.M. to 10 P.M.
